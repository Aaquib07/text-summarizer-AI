Frequently Asked Questions

What is a text summarizer?

A text summarizer uses AI to condense long documents, articles, or text into shorter, more manageable summaries while preserving the key information.

Can I summarize PDF files?

Yes! Upload any PDF or DOCX file and our AI will extract and summarize the text automatically. Files up to 10MB are supported.

What summary modes are available?

We offer three modes: Short (2–3 sentences), Detailed (3–5 paragraphs), and Bullet Points (key insights as a list).

Is the text summarizer free to use?

Yes, TextSummarizer AI is free to use. Simply paste your text or upload your file and get an instant summary.
